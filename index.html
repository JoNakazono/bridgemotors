About Bridge Motors

We introduce high-value, investment-worthy used cars located in Japan to car enthusiasts and affluent collectors in India. These vehicles already have well-established market values, making them reliable assets.

Japan has an abundance of well-maintained, high-quality used cars. Thanks to meticulous local care and the current yen depreciation, these vehicles can often be acquired at favorable prices.

In addition to the featured inventory, we are able to source specific vehicles upon request. Cars can be presented either online or in person in Japan.

If you choose to visit Japan, we can also arrange a personalized travel itinerary alongside your vehicle viewing. We provide full support throughout the purchasing process.

Due to Indian import laws, used cars must be at least 50 years old to be imported. Until then, we offer secure, long-term storage in our dedicated Japanese warehouse.
